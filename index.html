About the Project

The GlobalFarm Market Platform was born out of a pressing need to help local farmers in Nigeria and Africa reach a global audience. Many of these farmers struggle with showcasing their products due to limited access to international markets. This project aims to bridge that gap by creating a user-friendly platform where local farmers can list their products and have them ordered by global customers.

This platform is a Portfolio Project developed for ALX, showcasing a real-world solution that helps local economies grow by connecting them to global buyers.
